FAQ

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the top-right corner of the page. Follow the prompts to provide your information and set up your account credentials.

To add a new property listing, log in to your account and navigate to the "My Properties" section. Click on the "Add New Property" button and follow the prompts to provide property details, images, and pricing information.

After logging in, go to the "My Properties" section. Here, you can edit or delete your existing property listings, update property details, images, and pricing, and manage availability dates.

Use the search bar on the homepage to specify your desired location, property type, rental duration, and other preferences. Click "Search" to view a list of available rentals that match your criteria.

Once you find a suitable rental, click on the listing to view more details. Click the "Book Now" button to initiate the booking process. Follow the steps to select rental dates, provide your information, and confirm the booking.
