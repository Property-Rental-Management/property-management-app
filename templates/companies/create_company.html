Company Details

Companies are used to organize Properties. A company represents a distinct entity or organization within your business. It can be a real estate agency, a property management company, or any other type of business entity that owns or manages properties.

By creating companies, you can categorize and manage your properties more efficiently. Each property can be associated with a specific company, allowing you to easily track and distinguish properties based on their ownership or management.

It is essential to define at least one or more companies under which your properties will be classified. This enables you to organize and streamline your property management processes effectively.

To get started, simply fill out the form on the left with the necessary company details, such as the company name, description, address, contact information, and website. Click the "Save & Proceed" button to create the company and proceed to the next steps.
